Breastfeeding Support

Breastfeeding can come easily to some and be more difficult for others. Your first child might take to it immediately and your next might struggle. Whatever your journey may hold there are a lot of support resources and groups available to help you.

Here at Mama & Cub there is no judgement. We are firm believers that fed is best!

Mindfulness and Wellbeing

It is so important to look after yourself. Practicing mindfulness can help us avoid becoming overwhelmed and to be more present in the moment. It can help us relax, sleep, and manage our feelings in a more gentle way.
